I'm a student at École Polytechnique. I'm currently doing a research internship with [Lorenzo Rosasco](https://web.mit.edu/lrosasco/www/) on operator learning.

I'm interested in the mathematical side of machine learning — functional analysis, stochastic calculus, Markov chains, statistical learning theory — and more broadly in Scientific Machine Learning (SciML). I also enjoy working on concrete problems: I've spent some time on epistemic uncertainty estimation in computer vision, looking at how MC-Dropout and Deep Ensembles hold up under adversarial attacks.

I'm looking to work in AI research long-term.
